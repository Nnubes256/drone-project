[−][src]Constant icaros_control::gamepad::THROTTLE_QUICK_TRIGGER_BOUND

const THROTTLE_QUICK_TRIGGER_BOUND: f32 = 0.1;

A constant factor that determines how much a full press of one of the triggers may influence the final throttle, in percentage terms (i.e. 0.1 means it may influence the throttle up or down up to a 10% of the total value range).
